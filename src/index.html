Companies in the Mist

"Companies in the Mist" is an analog murder mystery game where six players assume the roles of different characters on a pirate team led by Captain Kidd. The game is set in a mysterious sea of mist and the players will encounter various puzzles and terrifying events. To survive and escape, the players must collaborate to uncover the truth behind the mysteries and determine who among them is an ally and who is an enemy. This game combines elements of strategy, role-playing, and detective work to create an engaging and thrilling experience.

Roots

Descend into a spellbinding journey that distorts the familiar into something eerily unfamiliar with Roots -- a 3D narrative adventure game that navigates the depths of fear and self-doubt. Take on the role of a desperate but determined protagonist, stranded by a blown tire in the dead of night with little but a fickle flashlight and her own thoughts to guide her. Amongst only trees and darkness, she has no choice but to confront her paralyzing phobias and overcome the obstacles that lay before her.
